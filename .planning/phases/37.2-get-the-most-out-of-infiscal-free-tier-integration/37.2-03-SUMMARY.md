## SHOULD-Differ Secrets (Task 2)

| Secret | Status | Notes |

### Intentionally Same Across Environments (Safe to Share)

- `PORT` — 3000 in all environments; not a secret
- `LOG_LEVEL` — `warn` in all environments; not a secret
- `RESEND_FROM_EMAIL` — same sending address across environments
- `REDIS_URL` — prod has Render Redis; dev/staging omit it (falls back to in-memory rate limiting)

## Key Fix: Prod DATABASE_URL

The most critical fix: prod `DATABASE_URL` was pointing to the local development PostgreSQL instance (`postgresql://ourcomputer@localhost/secureshare_test`), which would cause all production DB operations to fail. Updated to the Render internal PostgreSQL connection string. Render Secret Sync will auto-propagate to the service.
