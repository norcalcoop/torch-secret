## Accomplishments

- `createCopyButton` accepts new optional third param `options?: { autoClearMs?: number }`. When set, starts a `setInterval` countdown after each successful copy, labels the button "Copied — clears in Ns", and calls `navigator.clipboard.writeText('')` at zero. Re-copy resets interval. Reduced-motion: label silent, clipboard still cleared.
- `createExpirationSelect` accepts new optional third param `suggestion?: { value: string; reason: string }`. Authenticated users with a suggestion see a `<p>` hint below the dropdown. Anonymous branch unchanged (returns early before hint logic). `getValue()` closure preserved through outer wrapper div.
