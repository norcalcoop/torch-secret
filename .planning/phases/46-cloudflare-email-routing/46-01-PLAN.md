  <action>Human action required in Cloudflare dashboard. Claude cannot access the dashboard directly.

  Complete these steps in order. Do not skip ahead — each step depends on the previous.

  PRE-FLIGHT DNS CHECK (run in terminal first):
    dig MX torchsecret.com +short
    dig TXT torchsecret.com +short
  Expected: empty output (no MX, no TXT records). If any MX records are returned, do NOT proceed — pause and investigate what email service is currently configured. Research confirmed clean slate as of 2026-03-04 but verify live before proceeding.

  STEP 1 — Enable Email Routing:
  1. Log in to dash.cloudflare.com
  2. Select the account containing torchsecret.com
  3. Click "Websites" in the left nav → select torchsecret.com
  4. In the left sidebar, click "Email" → "Email Routing"
  5. Click "Get started" or "Enable Email Routing"
  6. Cloudflare shows DNS records it will add: 3 MX records (amir/linda/isaac.mx.cloudflare.net) and 1 SPF TXT record (v=spf1 include:_spf.mx.cloudflare.net ~all)
  7. Click "Add records and enable" — do NOT add these records manually; let Cloudflare add them
  8. If Cloudflare prompts about existing MX records, accept the deletion prompt

  STEP 2 — Verify torch-secret@gmail.com as destination:
  1. In Email Routing, click the "Destination addresses" tab
  2. Click "Add destination address"
  3. Enter: torch-secret@gmail.com
  4. Click "Send verification email"
  5. IMMEDIATELY open torch-secret@gmail.com inbox — check BOTH inbox AND spam folder
  6. Find the email from Cloudflare with subject similar to "Verify your email address for Cloudflare Email Routing"
  7. Click "Verify email address" in that email
  8. Return to the Cloudflare dashboard — status for torch-secret@gmail.com should now show "Verified"

  If the verification email does not arrive within 3 minutes: check spam folder, then use "Resend email" in the Cloudflare dashboard. Wait 2-3 minutes between resend attempts (Cloudflare enforces a rate limit — Error Code 2025 if you resend too quickly).

  STEP 3 — Create 7 routing rules:
  Navigate to Email Routing → "Routing rules" tab. For each address below, click "Create address" and fill in:
  - Custom address field: enter ONLY the prefix (e.g., hello — NOT hello@torchsecret.com; Cloudflare appends the domain automatically)
  - Action: "Send to"
  - Destination: torch-secret@gmail.com (select from dropdown)
  - Click Save

  Create rules for these 7 prefixes (any order):
  1. hello
  2. contact
  3. admin
  4. info
  5. support
  6. security
  7. privacy

  After creating all 7 rules, confirm all 7 show "Active" status in the Routing rules tab. If any show "Pending", the destination address was not verified — complete Step 2 first.</action>
